About Me

I'm an aspiring Web Developer based in in my hometown of Vestal, NY, currently enrolled in Bellevue University's Web Development cohort. Having grown up during the rise of the internet becoming a central touchstone of culture across the world, I suppose it's no surprise that I found myself drawn towards the art of designing the ins-and-outs of websites. There's a certain satisfaction to witnessing a completed page or application come together from your own efforts, it keeps me eager to pursue my goal of becoming a Full-Stack Developer upon graduation.

When I'm not mulling over HTML, JavaScript, and how to get that CSS padding just right, in my spare time, my main hobbies are writing fiction novels that I sure hope are good, going jogging on the local trails (see photo), and playing video games. A few of my favorite games include The Legend of Zelda series, Final Fantasy XIV, and Super Smash Bros. Ultimate.
